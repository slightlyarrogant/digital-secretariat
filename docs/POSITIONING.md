# Positioning and sales message

## Category

**The communication control plane for AI-operated companies.**

Digital Secretariat is not positioned as a smart inbox, a generic dashboard, or an autonomous
assistant. Those categories understate the risk. In a company operated by multiple agents,
automations, legacy jobs, and humans, client communication is where internal inconsistency becomes
an external promise.

## Core message

**One company. One client interface. No invisible promises.**

Digital Secretariat gives every agent and operator one shared account of:

- what the client sent;
- what the company sent;
- which exact revision was approved;
- what the company promised;
- who owns the resulting obligation and deadline;
- what requires a decision now;
- what delivery and audit evidence actually exists.

## The commercial problem

Without a communication control plane, several systems can speak for the same company from
different state. A half-working legacy module can continue a long prospect conversation. Another
agent can offer different terms. A deadline can exist in an email but nowhere in operations. A
generated obligation can contradict the agreement because the system did not know the source facts.

These are not isolated “AI hallucinations.” They are governance failures:

1. More than one uncontrolled path can communicate externally.
2. Communication does not update shared organizational state.
3. Commitments have no mandatory owner, deadline, source, or audit trail.
4. Delivery is assumed from intent instead of proved by the send rail.

AI increases the speed and volume of these failures. It does not create the underlying problem.

## Product promise

Digital Secretariat makes a disciplined operating model enforceable:

1. Every inbound client interaction is registered with its source and context.
2. Every outbound message uses one canonical rail with policy, revision, approval, and delivery
   evidence.
3. Every client commitment becomes visible shared state with an owner and deadline.
4. Every agent reads the same client truth before proposing an action.
5. The owner sees exceptions and decisions rather than every raw event.

## Proof, integration, and roadmap

Keep sales claims aligned with evidence.

### Implemented in the reference distribution

- inline inbound email content and source mailbox context;
- signed human decisions on drafts and replies;
- canonical mail release with stale-revision and newer-inbound controls;
- append-only send evidence and visible failure states;
- cases/obligations, operational attention, and communication measurements;
- private owner access through the Tailscale identity boundary.

### Requires company-wide integration

- disabling or routing every legacy SMTP and client-messaging path through the rail;
- mapping every relevant mailbox, portal, messaging channel, and source system;
- making obligation creation mandatory for all commitment-bearing workflows;
- enforcing network egress so an old process cannot bypass the canonical gateway.

### Do not claim as automatic today

- perfect interception of every customer channel;
- perfect extraction of every promise or deadline;
- autonomous external communication without human or explicit policy approval;
- elimination of all model errors;
- a universal no-configuration installation for any company.

## Message hierarchy

1. **Risk:** In an AI company, several systems can speak for the company at once.
2. **Consequence:** Clients receive promises the rest of the company cannot see or fulfill.
3. **Control:** Digital Secretariat creates one observable client interface.
4. **Mechanism:** Shared inbound context, one outbound rail, explicit obligations, and evidence.
5. **Outcome:** The company can scale AI operations without scaling invisible commitments.

## Recommended copy

### Headline

**One company. One client interface. No invisible promises.**

### Subheadline

**Digital Secretariat is the communication control plane for AI-operated companies. It keeps every
client message, approved response, commitment, deadline, and delivery record in one shared
operational truth.**

### Short pitch

**When several agents and automations can talk to clients, the inbox is no longer the problem.
Coordination is. Digital Secretariat ensures that external communication follows one controlled rail
and that every promise becomes visible company state.**
