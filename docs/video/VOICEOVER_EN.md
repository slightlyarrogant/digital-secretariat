# Voiceover script — English adaptation

Use this only after the Polish master timing is locked. It is an adaptation, not a literal subtitle.

**A growing company creates more inboxes, deadlines, documents, and places where something can be
missed. Digital Secretariat does not add another stream of information. It shows the owner the few
items that genuinely need a decision today.**

**Every inbound item keeps its context. Open the email in place and see the sender, receiving inbox,
message, attachments, and company relationship without jumping between systems. Write a short reply
beside the message. A human approves it; the controlled rail protects the revision, recipient, and
thread. Only recorded delivery counts as sent.**

**The outcome is measurable: response time, automatic and template-assisted handling, delivery,
bounces, and campaign conversion. Not an isolated KPI, but a trend and its cause.**

**The owner gets the same private workspace on laptop and phone through Tailscale identity, while
database and sending privileges stay separate. An AI agent can lead installation: inspect the host,
diagnose an error, propose a reversible fix, and record evidence. Humans still approve changes to
data, network access, and outbound mail.**

**This is not a bot promising to do everything. It knows what awaits a decision, presents the facts,
and safely executes the approved action. Digital Secretariat. Order, evidence, decisions.**
